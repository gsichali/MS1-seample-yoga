About Us


Seample Yoga is at the heart of Dublin and was founded by Grace Sichali. Yoga classes can be dynamic, alignment focused, strengthening, still and restorative, or anything in between. Our yoga instructors offer a variation of poses and movements allowing you to work at your own pace and ability. For all Yoga classes we supply Yoga mats. But you’re welcome to bring your own if you’d prefer. Seample offers a variety>of classes which can be daunting for a beginner but we provide the best guidance for you. The studio also offers yoga for pregnant women and free massage every other month for members. Yoga Studios offers services such as mindfulness, relaxation and general well-being classes. The studios are also available to rent for group bookings.
