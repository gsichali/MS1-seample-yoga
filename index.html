Let yoga breed a spiritual wonderland for you and me

Founded by Grace Sichali, Seample Yoga is based in the heart of Dublin city, Ireland. Seample Yoga has a variety of classes from beginner to intermediate with the most experienced instructors on the Island. The studio also offers yoga for pregnant women and free massage every other month for members. Yoga Studios offers services such as mindfulness, relaxation and general well-being classes. The studios are also available to rent for group bookings.

What is Yin Yoga?


Yin Yoga is a slow-paced style of yoga as exercise, incorporating principles of traditional Chinese medicine, with asanas that are held for longer periods of time than in other style.
