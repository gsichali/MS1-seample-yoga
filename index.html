Welcome to Seample Yoga


Founded by Grace Sichali, Seample yoga is based in the heart of Dublin city, Ireland. Seample Yoga has a variety of classes from beginner to intermediate with the most experienced instructors on the Island. The studio also offers yoga for pregnant women and free massage every other month for members.

Yoga Studios offers services such as mindfulness, relaxation and general well-being classes. The studios are also available to rent for group bookings.
